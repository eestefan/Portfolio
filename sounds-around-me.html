THE SOUNDS AROUND ME

First Year Grad School Project in Creative Coding, 2019

Visit to Play

The Sounds Around Me is a self-illustrated educational flipbook for small children ages 3-5. It is intended to help children experience and learn animals and animal sounds in their surrounding and outside environments. Ideally this would be a tablet app for easy accessibility. This is my final project created in the University of Miami Interactive Media "Creative Coding" class using p5.js.
