ABOUT

This is a game where the player must maneuver the cat loving robot across a rotating obstacle road to get to its kitty cat on the other side of the street. It is meant to provide a fun activity to younger audiences and those young at heart, while giving the player challenges in coordination and strategy. The concept came about from Yan’s love of games, my curiosity with motion and our shared love of cats.

Materials and Software Used: Robot—littleBits: Power Bit, codeBit, 2 DC Motors, 2 Sliders, split wire, 4 wire bits, 2 wheels, 2 mounting boards; Kitty Cat—littleBits: Power Bit, codeBit, LED Matrix, speaker, proximity sensor, wire bit, 2 mounting boards; The Road—littleBits: Power Bit, dimmer switch, branch bit, 2 servos, XL mounting board; Other Materials: Cardboard, Paint, Artist Tape, Misc construction materials, Cardstock paper.
