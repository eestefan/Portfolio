ABOUT

The “Visual Learning Center” is a learning tool for children with autism. It is a virtual reality environment meant to act as a supplemental study tool to reinforce lessons learned in the classroom. The initial of events in this prototype mimic the learning process within the classroom, followed by extending the process to virtual real-world environments to help students connect the lesson with their everyday life.

The prototype video shows a draft of how students will interact with this VR tool.
