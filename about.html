ABOUT

Elizabeth has worked as a graphic designer for two decades, currently working as Senior Graphic Designer and Project Manager at Miami Law at the University of Miami. Before joining Miami Law in 2010, Elizabeth worked more than a decade as sole in-house graphic designer for the Miami-based nonprofit National YoungArts Foundation. There as Manager of Communications and Publications, she was involved in print, web and media relations initiatives, including branding, marketing and advertising, and served on the executive editorial staff for its YoungArts magazine. Additionally, she has worked several years as a book interior designer for Outskirts Press, Inc.

She holds a B.F.A with a concentration in sculpture and minor in art history from Florida International University. And Elizabeth is currently working towards an M.F.A in Interactive Media at University of Miami.
